Functional requirements

1. The system shall provide a list of the open attractions filtered by:
	- name;
	- distance from the user;
	- category (Fun, Utility, Restaurants, Shop, Show);
	- estimated waiting time of the queue,
ordered by distance and waiting time.

2. The system shall calculate the park attraction waiting time every time there is a report for the attraction.

3. The system shall provide a wishlist for logged park visitors.
